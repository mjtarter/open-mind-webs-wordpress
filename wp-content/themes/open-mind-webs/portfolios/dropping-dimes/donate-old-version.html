A Player's Message to the Fans

The crowd has left the building
but the memories will still live on
like a symphony
playing, with heart filled emotion,
that one special song

No more introductions,
no more calling out our names..
the only thing we have left will be
those wonderful memories of the Game

Old players and old loyal fans
remembering all the good times we had
as we played our hearts out on the floor
and they filled All the stands
Passion filled the building
from the floor to the last seat up top

We gained extra energy from our fans
and our opponents knew our collective motors
couldn't be stopped,
so empowering was their energy
in ways that can never be told

WE won three championships together
and all on the road,
the crowd has left the building
and we'll follow behind and
Everyone knew one thing for Certain,
as we played together,
we put it all on the line

Thank YOU.

Mel Daniels
HOF 2012
