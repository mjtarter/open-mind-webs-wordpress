ABA Members to the Hall of Fame

The ABA, and more specifically the 3-time ABA champion Indiana Pacers have received some long overdue respect in recent years. Below is a list of the former members of the ABA that have been inducted into the Naismith Hall of Fame since 2011.

  • 2011 Artis Gilmore (Kentucky Colonels)
  • 2012 Mel Daniels (Indiana Pacers)
  • 2013 Roger Brown (Indiana Pacers)
  • 2014 Bobby “Slick” Leonard (Coach, Indiana Pacers)
In recent years there has been increased exposure to what these players meant to the game of basketball and what it is today, as well as the hardships they faced playing in a league that paid its players nowhere near what players are paid today. Among this exposure, a recent documentary by film producer Ted Green explores the amazing stories behind the life of recent hall of fame inductee, Roger Brown in Undefeated: The Roger Brown Story. Mr. Green is also releasing a documentary entitled “Bobby "Slick" Leonard: Heart of a Hoosier” that gives an in depth look into the fascinating life and basketball career of another recent hall of fame inductee, Slick Leonard.

It is often said that an artist's work is never truly appreciated until after their death, and so can be said with the ABA, but better late than never.
