ABA Legacy

Rick Barry was the first big-name player to make the switch from the NBA to the ABA. Named to the “50 Greatest Players in History” by the NBA, he was the only player to ever be the season leader in scoring in the NCAA, NBA, and ABA. His sons have carried on the legacy with Jon, Brent, and Drew all having played in the NBA. Rick and Brent even became only the second father-son duo to win NBA championships as players. The family has combined to play over 40 seasons of professional basketball and even played for many of the same franchises. Rick, John, and Brent all played for the Houston Rockets, and Rick, Jon, and Drew played for the Golden State Warriors.

Some other former ABA players with sons who also played professional ball include:

  • Wayne Chapman (Rex Chapman)
  • Rich Dumas (Richard Dumas)
  • Earle Higgins (Sean Higgins)
  • Walt Piatkowski (Eric Piatkowski)
  • Al Salvadori (Kevin Salvadori)
  • Walter Szczerbiak (Wally Szczerbiak)
  • Collis Temple (Garrett Temple)
  • David Vaughn Jr (David Vaughn III)
