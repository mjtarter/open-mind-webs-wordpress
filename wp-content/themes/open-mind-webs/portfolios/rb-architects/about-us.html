R&B ARCHITECTS, LLC

R&B Architects, LLC is a knowledge and solutions driven company with developed expertise in Architecture, Community Planning, New Construction, Adaptive Re-Use, Remodel, and Historic Preservation. We create solutions that transform our client’s needs into marketable assets enhancing the communities in which we all live.

Our firm is built on enthusiasm for exceptional design and the technical capabilities required to create buildings that are appropriate for our client’s program and that are environmentally sustainable. We are attentive listeners, carefully transforming your words into images and built environments. Our diligence during the creative process results in realistic solutions.
