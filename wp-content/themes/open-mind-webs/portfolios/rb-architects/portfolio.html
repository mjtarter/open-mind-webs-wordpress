Project Description

The Franklin Development Corporation was established in 2009 by former Franklin City Council-person Craig Wells to aid in the physical and economic growth of Frank- lin’s downtown core. In 2010, the Development Corporation hired Brent Mather to study the East Jefferson Street Block for façade renovations and 4 Second Floor Con- dominiums over existing retail. Working with four building owners, multiple tenants, the city planning department, and Franklin Mayor, Brent proposed an initial $200,000 restoration of the Richardsonian Romanesque building. This included masonry rehabilitation, window restoration and replacement, and retail storefront improvements.

+ Client:

Franklin Development Corporation

+ Construction Budget:

$12.8 Million

+ Professional Services:

Urban Planning and Master Planning, Historic Preservation, Schematic Design, Design Development, Construction Documentation, Construction Administration

+ Awards:

2010 Franklin Development Outstanding Contribution to Historic Preservation
